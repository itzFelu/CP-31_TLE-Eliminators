if there are consecutive 3 empty cells, then the answer will be 2, because we can fill any number of cells with those.

Example
. . . # . .
* . * # . . <-- filled 2
* * * # . .
* . * # * .
* * * # * .
* . * # * *
* * * # * *


if there are less than 3 consecutive cells, answer will be no of empty cells
